## Hero Image

- Purpose: README hero, WordPress hero, and first public project impression.
- Dimensions: 2400 x 1350.
- Existing asset: `assets/hero/hero-image.png`.
- Style direction: premium route-map infrastructure, public/private boundary,
  warm paper records, and reviewed business pathways.
- Color direction: off-black base, emerald route/action signals, gold trade
  lines, red review accents, warm paper.
- Typography direction: bold modern display type, minimal text, high contrast.
- Canva prompt: Create a cinematic editorial hero image for Black2Africa, a
  reviewed Black US-Africa business network. Use an off-black infrastructure
  background, emerald route markers, gold trade lines, warm paper record shapes,
  and a protected folder or route-map motif. Make it premium, strategic, human,
  and memorable. No private dashboards, no readable personal data, no legal
  forms, no generic SaaS template.
- Alternate Canva prompt: Design a public project hero for Black2Africa showing
  trusted diaspora business pathways, reviewed opportunities, and protected
  ownership work. Use dark infrastructure texture, route-map geometry, gold and
  green signals, and clean editorial spacing.
- Alt text: Black2Africa hero image showing protected route infrastructure for
  reviewed Black US-Africa business pathways.
