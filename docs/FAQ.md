# FAQ

## Is Black2Africa open source?

No. This public surface is not an open-source release. All rights are reserved.

## Does this repository include the working engine?

No. The engine, source code, deployment workflow, private records, and
operational materials remain private.

## Can I reuse the brand or idea commercially?

No. Commercial use requires written permission.

## Does Auntie AI file legal documents automatically?

No. Auntie AI is described publicly as owner-controlled preparation support. Any
official filing, signature, declaration, payment, MFA, identity verification, or
final submission remains owner-controlled.

## Can I contribute code?

Not through this public surface. Suggestions may be reviewed, but public code
contributions are not accepted here.

## Why publish a protected surface at all?

To make the project legible for review, portfolio, partnership, sponsorship, and
public storytelling without giving away the private system.

## What should I read first?

Read `PROJECT_BRIEF.md`, `PUBLIC_PRIVATE_BOUNDARY.md`, and
`COMMERCIAL_USE_POLICY.md`.
