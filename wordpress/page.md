## What This Project Is

Black2Africa is a business and trade network concept with a working private
implementation behind it. The public story focuses on reviewed opportunities,
partner pathways, sponsor interest, founder support, and owner-controlled
preparation for copyright, trademark, patent, publishing, and evidence work
through the Auntie AI lane.

This is not a public data room and not an open-source release.

## Why It Matters

Black builders and diaspora partners often need more than visibility. They need
context, trust, review, receipts, and protection from extractive collaboration.

Black2Africa is designed to help people move from interest to a reviewed next
step without exposing private ownership records or pretending that complex
legal, financial, or operational work should be handled casually.

## How It Works

The public flow is simple:

1. A visitor arrives with a partnership, opportunity, sponsor, or ownership
   support need.
2. Public-facing material explains the path and sets expectations.
3. Reviewed intake separates public-safe interest from private records.
4. Approved public outputs can become project pages, directories, briefs, or
   education materials.
5. Protected owner work stays in private systems until an owner-controlled
   portal, professional, or filing handoff is appropriate.
