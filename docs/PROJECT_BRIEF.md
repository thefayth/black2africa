# Project Brief

## Project name

Black2Africa

## Project slug

black2africa

## Short description

Black2Africa is a reviewed Black US-Africa business network and protected
owner-support platform. It helps route opportunities, partnerships, sponsor
interest, founder support, and owner-controlled IP preparation without exposing
private records.

## Purpose

The project exists to create a trustworthy bridge between public opportunity
discovery and private review. It is designed for people who need both access and
protection: builders, sponsors, organizers, creators, and operators moving work
across communities and borders.

## Product pillars

- Reviewed Black US-Africa business opportunities
- Partner and sponsor intake
- Public directory and resource surfaces
- Private dashboard and owner records
- Auntie AI support for copyright, trademark, patent, publishing, evidence, and
  digital hygiene preparation
- Receipt-based, owner-controlled handoffs

## Public proof-of-concept

This public surface communicates the concept, ownership posture, and product
direction. It does not release the engine.

## Relationship to Fantasia

Fantasia is used as a structural model for this public surface: clear README,
docs-first communication, protected commercial posture, and explicit
public/private boundaries.
