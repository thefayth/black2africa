# Brand Style Notes

Black2Africa should feel like infrastructure with taste: trade routes, business
intelligence, reviewed introductions, private ownership support, and diaspora
commerce without generic SaaS gloss.

## Brand Direction

- Tone: credible, warm, direct, owner-protective, and high-trust.
- Visual world: off-black infrastructure, emerald route/action states, gold
  trade value, deep red review boundaries, warm paper for ledgers and packets.
- Texture: map lines, route nodes, premium document surfaces, atlas logic, and
  editorial business systems.
- Avoid: generic startup gradients, stock handshakes, charity-coded Africa
  imagery, fake legal seals, government cosplay, and screenshots of private
  workflows.

## Typography Notes

Use a modern system stack or a similar rounded technical display face:

```css
font-family: "Sora", "Outfit", "Cabinet Grotesk", system-ui, sans-serif;
```

Ledger and receipt details can use:

```css
font-family: "JetBrains Mono", "IBM Plex Mono", ui-monospace, SFMono-Regular, Consolas, monospace;
```

## Image Direction

Use documentary business imagery, trade-route maps, founder/operator desks,
ports, markets, labs, studios, ledgers, packet folders, route nodes, and
reviewed-introduction visuals.

No sensitive document text should be readable in any image. No private
dashboard, admin panel, legal filing portal, or account-console screenshots
should appear in the public repo.
